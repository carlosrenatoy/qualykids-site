# Footer Component Spec

Target: Qualykids footer blocks.

## Pink Contact Band

- Pink background: `#e77c80`.
- White separator above.
- Centered white Qualykids logo.
- Two columns on desktop and mobile screenshot, with larger mobile text.

## Dark Footer

- Background: `#143a43`.
- Content width: 1150px desktop.
- White logo at top left.
- YouTube thumbnail spans content width on desktop, constrained on mobile.
- Circular black play overlay centered over thumbnail.
- Contact lines use pink icons and white text.
- Yellow UFO appears in the lower mobile footer.
